Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority at PulseHealth.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you manually submit on pulse-health-iota.vercel.app during registration, consultation booking, or file uploads (such as prescriptions or medical reports). This includes your name, contact details, and health information shared strictly for consultation purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide you with seamless Rs. 200 flat-fee consultations. We also use it to facilitate prescriptions, manage your ongoing consultations, and fulfill medication deliveries through our partners if requested.

3. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to ensure that your medical records and conversation history are fully protected. Only your assigned doctor has access to the files you submit in the Ongoing Consultation portal.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please contact us through the Support pulse-health-iota.vercel.app page.
